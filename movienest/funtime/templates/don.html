Husharu

Description:

Husharu is a 2018 Indian Telugu-language action comedy film directed by Shiva Nirvana and produced by Dil Raju under his banner Sri Venkateswara Creations. The film stars Sharwanand and Ruhani Sharma, with Vennela Kishore, Naresh, and Subbaraju in supporting roles. The music was composed by Devi Sri Prasad.
